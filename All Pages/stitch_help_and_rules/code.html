How to Play Screwy Louie

The Goal of the Game

expand_more

The main goal is to avoid taking tricks that contain the 'Screwy Louie' card, which is the Queen of Spades. Players try to score the fewest points possible over several rounds.

Game Setup

expand_more

A standard 52-card deck is used. Cards are dealt one by one to each player until the deck is gone. The player with the Two of Clubs starts the first trick.

Playing a Round

expand_more

Players must follow the suit of the first card played in a trick if they can. The player who plays the highest card of the leading suit wins the trick. The winner of a trick leads the next one.

Scoring

expand_more

At the end of a round, each Heart card taken in a trick is worth 1 point. The Queen of Spades (Screwy Louie) is worth 13 points. The game ends when a player reaches 100 points; the player with the lowest score wins.

Tips for Beginners

expand_more

Try to get rid of high-value cards (especially Spades) early in the game to avoid taking the Queen. Pay attention to which cards have been played to anticipate what other players might have.
